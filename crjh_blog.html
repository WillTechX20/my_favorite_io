About

Here are some fun facts about CRJH:

  • The school has an ASIP program where Special Needs students can go to the ASIP room and calm down when they feel upset during class.
  • OKE, GME, and Rylander all partially feed into CRJH.
  • Unlike OKE, CRJH has a mix of male and female teachers.

Common Terms

Here are common terms that students should know and their definitions:

Copyright
The protection of work agianst plagiarism that allow only the authors to distribute copies of their work unless they give permission to someone else.
Plagiarism
The act of taking one's work and claiming it as your own without giving credit, regardless of if it was paraphrased or not.
Credit
A reference to one's work.
Paraphrasing
The act of rewording text without changing its central idea.
Cheating
To gain an unfair atvantage over another in a game, bet, or in learning.
Bullying
An intentional, repeated, and one-sided act of harm from one same person to another same person that hurts the target's feelings.
