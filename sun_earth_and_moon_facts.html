Sun Facts

Here are some facts about the sun:

  • The sun is the center of our solar system. All of our planets obrit around it.
  • The sun is made of gas.
  • The sun rotates at 4,467 mph.
  • The sun's suface temperature is 5,778 °K.
  • The sun's gravity is 274 m/s².
  • The sun's radius is 432,690 mi.

Earth Facts

Here are some facts about the earth:

  • The earth is our planet.
  • The earth has an atmoshpere.
  • The earth is made of rock.
  • The earth rotates at 15 mph.
  • The earth orbits the sun at about 67,000 mph.
  • The earth's suface temperature is different in many spots.
  • The earth's gravity is about 9.81 m/s².
  • The earth's radius is 3,958.8 mi.

Moon Facts

Here are some facts about the moon:

  • The moon is earth's only satellite.
  • The moon has little air but not enough to have an atmoshpere.
  • The moon is made of rock from the earth.
  • The moon rotates every 27 days, at a speed of 0.635 mps.
  • The moon orbits the earth at about 2,288 mph, every 27 days.
  • The moon's suface temperature is 280 °F.
  • The moon's gravity is about 1.62 m/s².
  • The earth's radius is 1,079.4 mi.
